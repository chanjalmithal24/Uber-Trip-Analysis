## Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze Uber trip data and gain meaningful insights into revenue generation, booking trends, customer preferences, payment methods, and vehicle performance. The analysis helps in understanding business patterns and supports data-driven decision-making through interactive dashboards.

## Steps Performed
- Collected raw Uber trip data from the source dataset
- Cleaned the data by handling missing values, duplicates, and formatting issues
- Performed data transformation and created relationships between tables
- Designed data models optimized for analysis in Power BI
- Created key performance indicators (KPIs) such as Total Revenue, Total Bookings, Surge Revenue Percentage, Average Trip Distance, and Average Trip Duration
- Developed DAX measures to enable dynamic calculations and slicer-based filtering
- Built an interactive Power BI dashboard with filters for date range, payment type, vehicle type, and trip time
- Used charts and visuals to present trends, comparisons, and distribution of trips

## Key Insights
- The dataset generated a total revenue of $1.6M from 103.7K completed bookings
- UberX emerged as the most preferred vehicle type among customers
- Uber Pay is the most commonly used payment method, followed by Cash
- Day-time trips contribute nearly 73% of total bookings, indicating higher demand during the day
- Revenue trends fluctuate across the month, reflecting changes in customer demand and surge pricing
